TAYLOR SWIFT

CAREER

During her career, Swift has won 11 Grammy Awards, 32 American Music Awards (most winning artist), 23 Billboard Music Awards (most winning female artists), 12 Country Music Association Awards, 8 Country Music Academy Awards, 2 Brit Awards and 1 Emmy Award. As a songwriter, she has been honored 7 times as "Songwriter/Singer of the Year" by the Nashville Authors' Association and appeared in the Songwriters Hall of Fame. She is also the youngest person to enter Rolling Stone's list of the 100 Greatest Musicians of All Time 2015. In 2019, Billboard ranked Swift at number eight on the All Time Greatest Artists Chart. In 2020, Billboard also ranked Swift at number 29 on the list of 100 greatest music video artists of all time.

Swift has sold over 50 million pure album copies. Her first five studio albums have all sold over 4 million copies in the United States. She is the first artist to have 4 studio albums sell 1 million copies in the first week in the US and is the artist with the third best-selling digital single in the United States to date, with a total of 120 million copies. Besides, she was the best-selling artist in the United States in 2010 with nearly 76 million copies and was the highest-paid female musician of the 2010s.

In 2020, she became the first female artist to have both the album (Fearless) and the song ("Shake It Off") certified Diamond by the RIAA. Swift was the top female touring artist of the 2010s, and her Reputation Stadium Tour (2018) became the highest-grossing US tour of all time.
